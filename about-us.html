ABOUT US


Festival.com was formed in May, 2008 to create one big city that celebrates creativity. Our mission is to promote a positive culture by spotlighting the best sides of humanity—from the lighthearted and fun to the thought-provoking and enlightening. Today, we receive an average of 5 million visitors coming to our site each month, looking for articles on art, design, photography, architecture, science, technology, environmental issues, and more.

Our aim is to contribute to a brighter future by showcasing the top creative minds in the world. We celebrate diversity and people of all ages because we believe that the most beautiful fabric is one that includes us all.
